Exploring How Economic Conditions Relate to Population Growth Across U.S. States

This interactive map allows users to explore economic differences across U.S. states. Hovering over a state reveals a pop-up with its population growth and selected economic variable, while the linked chart highlights that state’s position for easy comparison across all states.

Data Source: U.S. Census Bureau and Bureau of Economic Analysis (compiled dataset for educational use).
